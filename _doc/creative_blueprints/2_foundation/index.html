Why this exists

The problem today

Paper, phones, and chased clipboards

A small clinic's front desk runs on clipboards and manual reminder calls. Staff re-key handwritten forms, lose track of who's actually checked in, and eat the cost of every no-show a simple confirmation could have prevented — while an urgent symptom can sit unnoticed in the same waiting line as a routine visit.

The solution

One intake flow that knows what's urgent

Patients self-complete a mobile intake form. Every submission is screened first — urgent symptoms like chest pain go straight to nurse review, while everyone else lands in a live staff queue and gets an instant SMS confirmation. No clipboard, no reminder calls, and nothing urgent left waiting in line.

Screen → route → queue → confirm
